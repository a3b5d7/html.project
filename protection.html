Symptoms of coronavirus disease (COVID-19) The symptoms most commonly associated with COVID-19 are: Cold-like symptoms (such as a runny nose, sneezing and/or a sore throat) Coughing Shortness of breath or pneumonia An elevated temperature (up to 38 degrees) A fever (above 38 degrees) A sudden loss of smell or taste (without a blocked nose) What to do if you think you have coronavirus Stay home if you have any of the symptoms associated with COVID 19. Make an appointment for a coronavirus test. Avoid in-person social contact. Don’t have any visitors or visit anyone else, and don’t do any shopping. Call your doctor if your symptoms get worse, if you have a fever above 38 degrees or if you have trouble breathing.
